《乔布斯传》中看到这么两句话：

> 乔布斯表示，人类一直都在从超前的知识进步中获益，并且在使用超前者所研发出来的东西。‘能够创造出在人类经验和知识史上占有一席之地的东西，是一种美妙和令人欣喜若狂的感觉。

> 此刻的失败者终将胜利/因为时代在变。

---

当下时刻AI的出现，好像再一次给了每个个体展现自己的机会,脑子里的想法可以借助AI的帮助变成现实世界的可以看得见摸得着的东西，这是多么”美妙和令人欣喜若狂的感觉“。

此刻的你也许认为自己是个失败者或者说活的不够精彩，那么你依然有可能终将成为胜利者，过上你自己觉得有意义的日子，因为时代在变。

文明级变革的路已经开启，你自己的脑子里有没有想法，能不能把想法变成可执行的行动才是你能不能找到美妙感觉的关键。

---

I saw these two passages in Steve Jobs：

> We are constantly benefiting from advances that went before us and taking things that people before us developed, he said. “It’s a wonderful, ecstatic feeling to create something that puts it back in the pool of human experience and knowledge.

> For the loser now / Will be later to win / For the times they are a-changin。

The arrival of AI feels like it's handed each of us another chance to show what we're made of. With AI's help, an idea in your head can become something real and tangible in the world — what a wonderful, ecstatic feeling that is.

Maybe right now you think of yourself as a failure, or feel you haven't lived vividly enough. Even so, you can still turn out a winner in the end, living the days that feel meaningful to you — because the times are changing.

The road to civilization-scale change has already opened. Whether you have an idea of your own, and whether you can turn that idea into action you can actually carry out — that's what decides whether you'll get to feel that wonder for yourself.
