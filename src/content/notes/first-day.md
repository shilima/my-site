今天，在AI的帮助下，我花了一天时间上线了自己的个人网站，这在以前是想都不敢想的事情。

6月12日是我60岁生日，这个年纪还能做一件和自己专业完全不相干的事情，是送给自己的礼物也是时代的馈赠。

就像首页上显示的"A New Journey After Sixty"一样，这里将记录我之后生活的一切所思、所想、所做，在互联网上留下自己的痕迹，感恩时代……

---

Today, with the help of AI, I spent a full day getting my personal website online — something I never dared imagine before.

June 12th is my 60th birthday. To do something completely outside my field at this age feels like a gift to myself, and a gift from the times we live in.

Just as the homepage says — "A New Journey After Sixty" — this is where I'll record everything I think, feel, and do from here on. Leaving my own mark on the internet.

Grateful for this era.
